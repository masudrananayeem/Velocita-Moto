Overview

The Koenigsegg Jesko is one of the most advanced hypercars ever created. Named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, the father of the company's founder, this masterpiece combines extreme power, cutting-edge aerodynamics, and revolutionary engineering. Built to push the boundaries of speed and performance, the Jesko is designed to dominate both road and track.
